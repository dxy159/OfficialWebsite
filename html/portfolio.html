RichardBot

An official facebook messenger bot built with Node.js. This handy little pal comes with many different cool features such as connecting to a weather API, thus it is able to provide weather information in any city in the world! As an NBA fan, RichardBot is also able to provide all the latest NBA information. Through web-scraping, RichardBot can update you on NBA scores, statistics, standings, and can even alert you of a close game! Message '@richardbottt help' on messenger to test it out yourself.

Drug On Time

Drug On Time is a service that allows a simpler method of communication between doctors and patients. It allows doctors to prescribe medication for patients digitally through the web. The patient would be alerted for whenever he/she needs to take a medication through our Drug On Time iOS/Android application. The patient can accumulate points and in turn exchange for medication at a local drug store.
