About Me

I love programming, and I enjoy participating in hackathons on the occasion. My main interests in technology are iOS development and artificial intelligence. You can take a look at all of my personal projects in my Portfolio section. Currently, I do not have many projects up for display as I am still in the process of adding the finishing touches in most of my projects!
