Pong (With Boids)

Tired of your ordinary game of pong? Well now you can click anywhere and be amazed by the beautiful and colorful boids!

Controls:

Player 1: Up (W) Down (S)

Player 2: Up (⬆︎) Down (⬇︎)

Your paddle will gradually change color as a result of coming in contact boids. Collect more boids to win!
